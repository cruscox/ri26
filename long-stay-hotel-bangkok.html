Bangkok's Best Long Stay Hotel in Sukhumvit

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel is Sukhumvit's most practical choice for extended stays in Bangkok. Whether you're here for a week-long business trip, a month-long working holiday, or a multi-month expat assignment, the hotel's combination of kitchenette rooms, extended stay rates, no joiner charge, and BTS connectivity makes it uniquely suited for guests who need more than a brief stopover.

Unlike serviced apartments that rent only for a minimum 30 nights with up-front deposits, Royal Ivory offers the flexibility of hotel booking — cancel when plans change, extend when you want to stay longer, and access full hotel services (pool, restaurant, 24-hour front desk, laundry) without the administrative burden of a rental contract.

Best Room Types for Long Stays

Deluxe Room — The Long Stay Choice (42+ sqm)

The Deluxe room is designed for extended stays. At 42+ square meters with an integrated kitchenette, it gives long stay guests the ability to self-cater for some meals — a major cost saver over time. Key features:

  • 42+ sqm: Genuinely spacious for weeks or months of comfortable living
  • Kitchenette: Refrigerator, microwave, kettle, and basic cooking equipment — make your own breakfast and store groceries from Tops or Villa supermarket nearby
  • Desk workspace: Dedicated writing/work desk with power outlets — practical for remote workers and digital nomads
  • Wardrobe space: Full wardrobe for extended wardrobe requirements
  • Thai decor: Comfortable, aesthetically warm environment for longer habitation
  • Fast WiFi: High-speed connection throughout — essential for remote workers

Suite — For Premium Long Stays (45 sqm)

The Deluxe (45 sqm) is the ultimate long stay option — a full living area separate from the bedroom, bathtub, and premium Thai decor throughout. For executives on extended Bangkok assignments, or guests who want genuine apartment-level space with full hotel services, the Suite represents exceptional long-stay value at Bangkok prices.

Living in the Nana Area: Long Stay Practical Guide

Grocery Shopping

  • Tops Market (Asok/Terminal 21): 1 BTS stop — full supermarket with imported goods, fresh produce, international brands
  • Villa Market (Sukhumvit): Premium international supermarket, 10 min by BTS
  • 7-Eleven / FamilyMart: Multiple locations within 50-100m of hotel — snacks, basic supplies, meals
  • Local fresh market: Morning market within 10 minutes walk — fresh Thai produce at local prices

Healthcare

  • Bumrungrad International Hospital: 5 minutes by BTS (Nana → Asok, 1 stop) — world-class international hospital
  • Samitivej Hospital Sukhumvit: 15 minutes by BTS — top-tier private hospital
  • Boots Pharmacy: Multiple locations within 300m — international medicines, healthcare
  • Local clinics: Several affordable walk-in clinics within 500m of hotel

Banking & Money

  • Multiple ATMs: Bangkok Bank, Kasikorn, SCB within 100m of hotel
  • Currency exchange: Authorized exchanges on Sukhumvit within 300m
  • Kasikorn Bank branch: Account opening possible for long stay visa holders

Fitness & Wellness

  • Hotel outdoor pool: Free for all guests — swim daily year-round
  • Fitness centers: Several gyms within 5-10 min walk (1,500-3,000 THB/month membership)
  • Thai massage: Authentic massage shops on Soi 4 from 300-400 THB/hour
  • Benjakitti Park: 1 BTS stop away — 3km jogging path, free, beautiful

Visa Considerations for Long Stays

Thailand offers several visa options for extended stays. The most common for long-stay visitors are the Tourist Visa (60 days, extendable 30 more), the Non-Immigrant B Visa for business, and the Thailand LTR (Long-Term Resident) Visa for qualifying retirees and remote workers. Royal Ivory front desk staff can provide TM30 documentation required by Thai immigration when registering at a hotel as your place of residence.

Royal Ivory for Digital Nomads & Remote Workers

Bangkok has emerged as one of Asia's top digital nomad destinations — and Sukhumvit Nana is one of Bangkok's most practical bases for remote workers. Royal Ivory specifically suits the remote-working lifestyle:

  • High-speed WiFi: Reliable broadband throughout hotel, including in-room and pool area
  • Desk in every room: Dedicated work desk with power outlets — not just a decorative table
  • Kitchenette (Deluxe): Make your own coffee, store food — avoid daily restaurant trips on work mornings
  • BTS to co-working spaces: Multiple co-working spaces in Asok, Thong Lo, and Silom — all reachable in 1-5 BTS stops
  • Quiet room environment: Soi 4 side street means minimal noise interference during calls and focused work
  • No joiner charge: Freedom to work without social restrictions or unexpected charges
  • Monthly discounts: Long stay rates designed for the "work for a month" digital nomad model

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get extended stay rates at Royal Ivory?

Contact reservations directly at reservations@royalivory.com or call +66 2 656 7888. Specify your intended stay length and preferred room type. Weekly (7+ night) and monthly (30+ night) rates are available at significant discounts from standard nightly rates. Direct booking always provides better rates than OTA platforms for extended stays.

Is there a minimum stay length at Royal Ivory?

No minimum stay requirement. Royal Ivory accepts bookings from 1 night. However, for the best long stay rates, booking 7+ or 30+ nights directly with the hotel provides the most significant discounts. Flexible cancellation options are available — ask at booking.

Can I cook in my room at Royal Ivory Hotel?

Yes. The Deluxe rooms (42+ sqm) include a kitchenette with refrigerator, microwave, kettle, and basic cooking equipment. This is particularly valuable for long stay guests who want to self-cater for some meals. Superior rooms do not have kitchenettes but do have a refrigerator and kettle.

Does Royal Ivory provide TM30 documentation for Thai immigration?

Yes. As a registered hotel, Royal Ivory automatically registers guests with Thai immigration upon check-in (TM30 reporting requirement). If you require a stamped copy of your TM30 for visa extension or other immigration purposes, front desk staff can provide this documentation.
