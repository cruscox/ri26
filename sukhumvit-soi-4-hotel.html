Guide to Sukhumvit Soi 4

What is Sukhumvit Soi 4?

Sukhumvit Soi 4 is a short but significant side street in Bangkok's Sukhumvit district. Branching off the south side of Sukhumvit Road near BTS Nana Station, Soi 4 runs approximately 300 meters south before widening and connecting to the Nana area streets. The soi is accessible by foot from the BTS in 2 minutes and serves as the residential and hospitality backbone for the Nana visitor community.

Unlike the main Sukhumvit Road — loud with traffic, tuk-tuks, and pedestrian crowds — Soi 4 is genuinely quiet. Local vehicle traffic only, wide enough pedestrian paths, and the ambient sounds of a living Bangkok neighborhood rather than a highway. This is why savvy Bangkok travelers specifically look for Soi 4 accommodation.

Getting to Sukhumvit Soi 4 / Royal Ivory Hotel

From BTS Nana Station

  1. Exit BTS Nana Station (Exit 1 or Exit 2 facing Sukhumvit Road south side)
  2. Walk west (right) along Sukhumvit Road approximately 300 meters
  3. Turn left into Sukhumvit Soi 4 (Nana Tai)
  4. Royal Ivory Hotel is 50 meters on the right (No. 73)
  5. Total: 2 minutes walking

From Suvarnabhumi Airport

  • Airport Rail Link + BTS: Airport to Phaya Thai (30 min) → BTS to Nana (7 stops, 18 min) → walk to hotel (2 min). Total: ~55 min, ~100 THB
  • Grab (ride-hailing): Direct to hotel, 45-60 min depending on traffic. Approx. 350-450 THB via expressway
  • Metered taxi: Available at airport — similar time and cost to Grab plus expressway tolls (~80 THB)

From Don Mueang Airport

  • Bus + BTS: Bus A1/A2 to Mo Chit BTS (~40 min) → BTS 8 stops to Nana (22 min) → walk 2 min
  • Grab/taxi: Direct to hotel, 30-50 min. Approx. 250-350 THB

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hotel on Sukhumvit Soi 4?

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel at 73 Sukhumvit Soi 4 is the best hotel on Soi 4. It's the only full-service hotel on the soi, offering 90 rooms (32-80 sqm), outdoor pool, restaurant, no joiner charge, and 4.2/5 rating. Other Soi 4 accommodation consists of small guesthouses without pool or full-service facilities.

Is Sukhumvit Soi 4 safe at night?

Yes, Sukhumvit Soi 4 is generally safe at night. The street is well-lit, has active businesses throughout the evening, and serves an international tourist community. Normal precautions apply (be aware of surroundings, keep valuables secure). Royal Ivory Hotel provides 24-hour security and can advise on safe movement in the area.

How do I get to Sukhumvit Soi 4 from central Bangkok?

Take BTS Sukhumvit Line to Nana Station (E4). Exit the station and walk 300m west along Sukhumvit Road. Turn left into Soi 4. Royal Ivory Hotel is 50m on your right. Total time from BTS Siam: 10 minutes (4 stops). From the airport: Airport Rail Link to Phaya Thai then BTS to Nana — approximately 55 minutes total.
