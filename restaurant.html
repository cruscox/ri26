Basil @ Nana 4 & Diners Sukhumvit 4 RestaurantAuthentic Thai cuisine and Western classics prepared with the freshest locally sourced ingredients. Advance orders welcome.

Basil @ Soi 4

  • Features only locally sourced ingredients, guaranteeing fresh authentic Thai cuisine with a modern touch made to suit all tastes. Our cooks specialize in Thai cuisine and as local chefs, we are proud to present diverse flavors with different cooks.
  • Basil features local authentic cuisines alongside world-renowned Thai menu items. As the restaurant name suggests, we specialize in making the perfect stir-fried basil chicken with selectable spice levels - guests can choose from 'mild' to an adventurous 'extra hot'. Other menu highlights include green curry, tom yum soup, tom kha gai and much more.

Diners

  • Offers Western classic dishes featuring All Day Breakfast Menu with American Breakfast set, continental delights, eggs, toast and more. The Soup & Salad section surprises guests with delectable chicken soups and grand-scale Caesar salad.
  • The main course serves primarily steaks - chicken, pork, fish - and spaghetti combinations. Lastly, the diner's classic menu includes notable choices: the sizzling grilled ham and cheese sandwich, the fresh chicken & tomato sandwich, and our chef's favorite - the one and only Royal Ivory Club Sandwich.

DezBar

  • Serving guests from all walks of life, DezBar hosts both local gatherings and international visitors.
  • Featuring multiple internationally recognized alcohol brands, we also offer fresh seasonal fruit juices and blends.
  • In cooperation with Walls Ice Cream, DezBar offers guests multiple shake varieties including the Choco Shake (a scoop of both chocolate and vanilla ice cream blended with milk) and the Devil's Delight with Bailey's Irish Cream.

Popular Thai Dishes

  • Pad Kra Pao: Our signature basil chicken with customizable spice levels
  • Green Curry: Creamy coconut curry with fresh herbs and vegetables
  • Tom Yum Goong: Spicy and sour shrimp soup with lemongrass
  • Pad Thai: Classic stir-fried noodles with tamarind and peanuts
  • Massaman Curry: Rich curry with potatoes and peanuts
  • Som Tam: Fresh papaya salad with chili and lime

Western Favorites

  • All Day Breakfast: American and Continental breakfast options
  • Grilled Steaks: Chicken, pork, and fish selections
  • Pasta Dishes: Spaghetti with various sauce combinations
  • Sandwiches: Club sandwich, ham & cheese, chicken & tomato
  • Soups & Salads: Caesar salad and hearty chicken soups
  • Comfort Food: Western classics for familiar tastes
