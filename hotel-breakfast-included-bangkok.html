Bangkok Hotel with Breakfast Included — The Value Advantage

Breakfast in Bangkok can be a surprisingly significant daily cost. A simple hotel breakfast in the Sukhumvit area typically costs 200-400 THB per person if not included. Over a week's stay, that's 1,400-2,800 THB in additional daily spend — before you've left the hotel.

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel includes breakfast in the room rate for all room types. At from 1,200 THB/night for a 32 sqm Superior room with breakfast, pool access, no joiner charge, and a central Nana location, it delivers exceptional all-inclusive value compared to any comparable property in the area.

The Royal Ivory Thai Restaurant

Unlike many Bangkok budget hotels that offer a basic continental breakfast in a cramped lobby space, Royal Ivory has a full on-site Thai restaurant — operating for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Breakfast is served in this proper restaurant environment, not a converted meeting room.

Restaurant Overview

The Royal Ivory restaurant specialises in authentic Thai cuisine — the food that the hotel's Thai-style property philosophy extends to the dining experience. This isn't generic international hotel food. Guests consistently praise the restaurant as an unexpected highlight of their stay.

All-Day Dining

Beyond the included breakfast, the restaurant provides an on-site dining option throughout the day. For long-stay guests or those who prefer the convenience of eating at the hotel, having a full kitchen restaurant rather than just a breakfast counter is a meaningful quality-of-life advantage.

Room Options — Breakfast Included with All

Superior Room (32 sqm) — Breakfast Included

The entry-level room at Royal Ivory is 32 sqm — significantly larger than the typical Bangkok budget hotel room of 15-22 sqm. Thai-style decor, en-suite bathroom, air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, free WiFi. Breakfast included. Pool access included. From 1,200 THB/night. Best for solo travellers and short stays.

Deluxe Room (42+ sqm) — Breakfast Included

The Deluxe room adds a kitchenette (refrigerator, microwave, kettle) to the Superior's features. At 42+ sqm, it's a genuine apartment-style space ideal for longer stays. Even with the kitchenette, the included breakfast means guests start every morning taken care of. Ideal for 7+ night stays. From 1,800 THB/night.

Suite (80 sqm) — Breakfast Included

The Suite provides a full separate living area, premium Thai decor, and bathtub alongside the bedroom. At 80 sqm with breakfast included, pool access, and no joiner charge, this is premium value for the Nana Bangkok area. From 2,800 THB/night — exceptional for a proper suite in central Sukhumvit.

Breakfast Inclusion for Long-Stay Guests

For long-stay visitors — those staying 7 nights, two weeks, or longer — breakfast inclusion has an outsized impact. It's not just the financial saving (significant over a 30-night stay). It's the daily rhythm it creates: wake up, eat a proper breakfast at the hotel, then set out for the day. No need to source breakfast elsewhere, no early-morning food logistics.

Royal Ivory's Deluxe rooms with kitchenette are particularly popular among long-stay guests. The combination of included daily breakfast and kitchenette facilities means guests have complete flexibility — hotel breakfast when they want the full service experience, kitchenette for lighter meals throughout the day.
