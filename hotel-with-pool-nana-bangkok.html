Why a Pool Matters in Bangkok

Bangkok's climate makes a hotel pool far more than a luxury amenity. With average temperatures of 28-35°C year-round and high humidity for much of the year, an outdoor pool transforms the quality of an extended stay.

The Bangkok Heat Reality

  • March-May (hot season): 35-40°C daily, extremely humid — pool becomes essential recovery space
  • June-October (wet season): 28-34°C, heavy afternoon rain but warm — pool ideal for late morning swims
  • November-February (cool season): 25-32°C, pleasantly warm — best pool season, cooler evenings, morning swims comfortable

Guests who explore Bangkok temples, markets, and shopping districts through the heat of the day consistently rate the ability to return to the hotel pool in the afternoon as one of their most valued experiences. It converts what could be an exhausting city break into a genuinely restorative trip.

Pool as Cost Saver

Bangkok's rooftop and pool bars charge significant entrance or minimum spend fees (often 300-800 THB) to use their pool facilities for the day. With Royal Ivory's included pool access, guests avoid this cost entirely for every day of their stay.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a hotel with a pool near Nana Plaza Bangkok?

Yes. Royal Ivory Nana Hotel on Sukhumvit Soi 4 has an outdoor swimming pool and is just 200 meters (3 minutes walk) from Nana Plaza. It is one of the only budget 3-star hotels in the Nana area with a pool — most properties nearby lack pool facilities due to space constraints.

Is the pool at Royal Ivory Hotel free to use?

Yes. The outdoor pool at Royal Ivory Nana Hotel is free to all hotel guests — included in your room rate. There is no pool entry charge, minimum spend, or additional fee. All room types (Superior, Deluxe, Suite) include full pool access.

What are the pool hours at Royal Ivory Nana Hotel?

The pool is available to all guests daily, typically from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Bangkok's tropical climate means the pool is comfortable year-round. Morning (7-9 AM) and late afternoon (4-7 PM) are the most popular times — cooler temperatures and good light. Contact the hotel directly to confirm current hours.

Is Royal Ivory the only hotel with a pool in the Nana Bangkok area?

Royal Ivory is one of the very few budget 3-star hotels in the Nana Sukhumvit Soi 4 area with an outdoor pool. Larger luxury hotels on main Sukhumvit Road have pools, but at significantly higher rates. At the 3-star budget price point (from 1,200 THB/night), Royal Ivory's pool is a rare and distinctive feature in the Nana area.
