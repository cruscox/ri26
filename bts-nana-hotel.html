Bangkok Transport Tips for BTS Nana Guests

Getting a Rabbit Card (BTS Pass)

If you're staying 3+ days, a Rabbit Card (BTS stored-value card) saves time and money. Purchase at any BTS station including BTS Nana. Load value and tap in/out at every BTS gate. Stored value never expires. Cards cost 100 THB deposit + initial load.

MRT Connection at Asok

BTS Asok (1 stop from Nana) connects to MRT Sukhumvit Station via a short walkway. The MRT (subway) reaches areas not served by BTS: Chinatown (Hua Lamphong/Sam Yan), Chatuchak (interchange), and the new Blue Line extension to Bangkok's western districts. One transfer gives you access to the entire rail network.

Airport Transfers

  • Suvarnabhumi Airport: BTS from Nana to Phaya Thai (7 stops, 18 min) → Airport Rail Link to airport (29 min) — total ~50 min, ~65 THB
  • Don Mueang Airport: BTS from Nana to Mo Chit (8 stops) → taxi to Don Mueang (~30 min, 200-300 THB)
  • Grab taxi: Suvarnabhumi direct from hotel ~350-450 THB via expressway

Night Transport (After BTS Hours)

BTS operates 06:00-midnight daily. After midnight, Grab (ride-hailing app) is the most reliable option. From Royal Ivory's location on Soi 4, Grab pickups are quick and prices to most Bangkok destinations are 80-250 THB. Our front desk can assist with Grab bookings.
