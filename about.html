About Royal Ivory Nana HotelLearn about our property, facilities & services, and prime Sukhumvit Soi 4 location

Nana Hotel Description Getting to know Royal Ivory

Our friendly staff welcomes you to experience a refined ambiance in a cozy atmosphere. They are here to help make your stay a memorable occasion. Royal Ivory Nana Hotel features 90 spacious rooms and a Presidential Suite.

Featuring Superior Room, Deluxe King Room, and Executive Suites, our rooms are spacious, well-equipped, and designed in traditional Thai fashion giving guests a chance to really experience true Thai heritage. Extraordinary care has been taken with every step in preparing the room. No detail has been neglected to give you the warm feeling of home, and all are furnished with a full set of modern amenities - everything you expect from a quality hotel & resort.

"It's in a quiet area on Soi 4 and the pool was very refreshing on hot days... This hotel and staff are to me easily the best around Thailand."

We have a restaurant, bar, swimming pool, and also a pool table. Our staff welcomes you like a family member and we hope to do our best to make you smile on your every visit to Royal Ivory Hotel.

Nana Hotel ServicesRoyal Ivory services with a smile, featuring hotel amenities

Royal Ivory offers you a range of facilities from swimming pool to massages. During your stay with us you can swim, take a break with a massage or even work on your laptop using our Free Wireless Internet. Our tailor shop offers you custom-made suits and blouses that will support your needs.

We also have a travel agent that can give you plenty of ideas on which other stops you can visit in Thailand. Our limousine service offers pick-up and drop-off at the Airport at a low rate. Unlike other hotels, we do not have hidden additional guest charges (no-joiner charges).
