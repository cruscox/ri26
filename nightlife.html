Bangkok Nightlife Guide from Royal IvoryExplore Bangkok's premier entertainment districts from our prime Nana location

Nana Plaza 500 meters awayWalking distance from Royal Ivory Hotel

Nana Plaza (officially Nana Entertainment Plaza, abbreviated NEP) is a 3-story entertainment complex in Bangkok, rumored to be one of the world's largest entertainment districts. Located on Sukhumvit Road Soi 4 directly across from the Nana Hotel, it's within easy walking distance of both Royal Ivory Hotel and the BTS Skytrain's Nana Station.

The name originates from the prominent Nana family, with Lek Nana being the most notable member, who owns substantial property in the area and maintains significant influence in Thailand.

Royal Ivory Advantage: Walk to Nana Plaza in just 5 minutes from our hotel, then return safely to your comfortable room.

Source: Wikipedia

Soi Cowboy 1.5km awayJust 1 BTS stop from Royal Ivory Hotel

Soi Cowboy is a famous 400-meter entertainment street in Bangkok, Thailand. With approximately 40 bars along its length, it's similar to Nana Plaza and Patpong, catering primarily to international tourists and expatriates.

Located near Sukhumvit Road between Sukhumvit Soi 21 (also called Soi Asoke) and Soi 23, Soi Cowboy is within walking distance from the BTS Skytrain's Asok Station and the Bangkok Metro's Sukhumvit Station. The Grand Millennium Hotel is a nearby landmark.

How to Get There from Royal Ivory:

  1. Walk to BTS Nana or take our complimentary tuk-tuk (5 minutes)
  2. Take BTS to Asok Station (1 stop, 3 minutes)
  3. Walk to Soi Cowboy (2 minutes)
  4. Total journey: About 10 minutes

The entertainment venues follow the traditional Thai format, serving alcoholic drinks in a social atmosphere.

Source: Wikipedia

Royal City Avenue (RCA) 10km awayBangkok's premier clubbing destination

Royal City Avenue - commonly known as RCA - is the epicenter of Bangkok's clubbing scene and an officially designated nightlife zone, guaranteeing entertainment seven nights a week. Clubbing institutions like Route 66 and Slim are packed every night and reach capacity on weekends.

RCA offers incredible variety for visitors: enjoy international DJs, live concerts, and performances by popular local artists all in one night. The avenue is divided into four blocks: Block A (closest to Rama 9 Road) through Block D (leading to Petchaburi Road).

Important Transportation Note: RCA is too far from public transport stations to walk comfortably, so a taxi or motorcycle taxi is required. Our hotel staff can arrange reliable transportation for you.

Travel Time from Royal Ivory: 20-25 minutes by taxi (approximately 150-200 Baht)

Source: Bangkok.com

Khao San Road 10km away1km of diverse entertainment options

Khao San Road transforms at night: neon signs flicker to life, music blasts from streetside sound systems, and crowds gather to plan their evening adventures in this iconic Bangkok destination.

The variety is impressive - from the relatively upscale Silk Bar and Cinnamon to the quiet tranquility of the back-alley Hippie de Bar, or the authentic charm of kerbside cocktail shacks complete with plastic stools. There's something to suit every mood, taste, budget, and preference. Budget-conscious travelers even purchase beers from the local 7-Eleven and enjoy them street-side.

Getting There from Royal Ivory: Take BTS from Nana to National Stadium (4 stops), then taxi to Khao San Road (10 minutes, ~100 Baht). Alternatively, take a direct taxi from the hotel (25-30 minutes, ~200 Baht depending on traffic).

Source: Bangkok.com
