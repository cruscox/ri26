Staying Near Terminal 21 Bangkok — The BTS Solution

Terminal 21 is one of Bangkok's most popular shopping destinations — a quirky themed mall at the Asok-Sukhumvit intersection directly connected to Asok BTS station (E5). Hotels directly adjacent to Terminal 21 command a premium due to the prime Asok location. The smarter option: stay at BTS Nana (E4) — one stop away — and get significantly better value.

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel on Sukhumvit Soi 4 puts Terminal 21 8-10 minutes away (walk to BTS Nana, 1 stop to Asok, walk into the mall). In exchange for that 8 minutes, guests get a 90-room boutique Thai hotel with outdoor pool, breakfast included, no joiner charge, and spacious rooms (32-80 sqm) — at prices from 1,200 THB/night compared to 3,000-6,000 THB/night at comparable-spec hotels at Asok itself.

🛍 Royal Ivory → Terminal 21 Journey

Step 1: Free hotel tuk-tuk shuttle to BTS Nana Station — 300m, 2 minutes

Step 2: BTS Skytrain Nana (E4) → Asok (E5) — 1 stop, 2 minute ride

Step 3: Walk from Asok BTS Exit 3 into Terminal 21 directly — 1 minute

Total time: 8-10 minutes door to mall entrance

Cost: BTS fare approx. 20-25 THB each way

Terminal 21 Bangkok — What to Know

Terminal 21 opened in 2011 and remains one of Bangkok's most distinctive malls. Each floor is designed around a different international city, giving the whole building an airport terminal theme that's a genuine Bangkok curiosity.

Terminal 21 Floor by Floor

Floor Theme Highlights
G (Ground) Caribbean Main entrance, international fast food
M Caribbean Luggage, accessories, mid-range shops
1st Tokyo Fashion boutiques, Japanese-inspired stores
2nd London Fashion, British-themed decor
3rd Istanbul & Rome Mixed retail, homeware, décor
4th Paris Fashion, beauty, cosmetics
5th San Francisco Food, restaurants, casual dining
6th Hollywood Pier 21 Food Court — Bangkok's best budget food court
7th Hollywood Cineplex (SF Cinema)

Pier 21 Food Court

Terminal 21's Pier 21 food court on the 6th floor is one of the most famous food courts in Bangkok. Authentic Thai dishes from 30-80 THB — pad thai, boat noodles, mango sticky rice, green curry. Consistently rated among the best-value food courts in the city. Easily accessible from Royal Ivory in 10 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Royal Ivory Nana Hotel from Terminal 21?

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel is 1 BTS stop from Terminal 21. The hotel's free tuk-tuk shuttles guests to BTS Nana Station (300m, 2 minutes), the Skytrain takes 2 minutes to Asok (1 stop), and Terminal 21 is directly connected to Asok BTS exit. Total: approximately 8-10 minutes from hotel to Terminal 21 entrance.

What BTS station is Terminal 21 Bangkok on?

Terminal 21 is at Asok BTS Station (E5) on the Sukhumvit Line. It is also connected to Sukhumvit MRT Station (interchange at Asok). From BTS Nana (E4) where Royal Ivory is located, it is exactly 1 stop east. Journey time on the Skytrain: 2 minutes.

Is there a budget hotel near Terminal 21 Bangkok with a pool?

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel is a budget 3-star hotel 1 BTS stop from Terminal 21 with an outdoor swimming pool included free for all guests. From 1,200 THB/night including breakfast. Hotels directly at Asok/Terminal 21 typically charge 3,000-6,000 THB/night for equivalent facilities. Royal Ivory offers the best value for guests wanting Terminal 21 access with pool and breakfast.

Can I walk from Royal Ivory to Terminal 21?

Walking from Royal Ivory to Terminal 21 is possible but not recommended — it's approximately 900m along busy Sukhumvit Road and takes 12-15 minutes in Bangkok's heat. The BTS is faster (8-10 min total), air-conditioned, and more comfortable. The hotel provides a free tuk-tuk shuttle to BTS Nana Station, making the BTS journey the obvious choice.
