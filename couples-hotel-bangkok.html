Bangkok Hotel for Couples — What Actually Matters

Choosing a Bangkok hotel as a couple involves a different checklist than solo travel. Room size matters — a 15 sqm budget room that's fine for one becomes cramped for two. Privacy matters — you want to feel like guests, not suspects. Value matters — the "couple price" shouldn't be double the solo rate.

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel on Sukhumvit Soi 4 hits all three. Spacious Thai-style rooms from 32 sqm, a clear no extra guest charge policy, breakfast included for both, and an outdoor pool that elevates the stay — all from 1,200 THB/night. Here's why couples consistently choose Royal Ivory for their Bangkok base.

Room Space — The Foundation of a Good Couples Stay

Bangkok's budget hotel market is full of rooms under 20 sqm — fine for a solo traveller who just needs a bed, but genuinely uncomfortable for two people sharing for more than a night or two. Royal Ivory's smallest room (Superior) is 32 sqm — the equivalent of a European mid-range double.

Room Options for Couples

Superior Room — 32 sqm

A genuine double room at 32 sqm. Thai-style decor, full en-suite bathroom, air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, free WiFi. Enough space for two people to move around comfortably, unpack properly, and not feel on top of each other. Breakfast and pool included. From 1,200 THB/night.

Deluxe Room — 42+ sqm (Recommended for Couples)

The Deluxe room is the sweet spot for couple stays. At 42+ sqm it has genuine living space — room for a sitting area separate from the bed. The kitchenette (refrigerator, microwave, kettle) lets couples store drinks, snacks, and breakfast extras. For a 5-7 night Bangkok trip, this is the ideal room. Breakfast and pool included. From 1,800 THB/night.

Suite — 45 sqm (Romantic/Special Occasion)

The Suite is the premium couple experience at Royal Ivory. A full separate living area, bathtub, premium Thai decor, and 45 sqm of genuine space. For anniversaries, honeymoons, or simply a special Bangkok trip, the Suite delivers an experience far above its 3-star price point. Breakfast and pool included. From 2,800 THB/night.

Privacy Policy — No Extra Guest Charges

A common frustration for couples at Bangkok budget hotels is being treated as suspect rather than valued guests. Some hotels charge extra "double occupancy" fees, require separate registration for partners, or make the check-in process uncomfortable for non-married couples.

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel operates a straightforward privacy-first policy. The room is yours. Your partner is your guest. There are no extra guest charges, no separate registration requirements for partners, and no staff interference. The hotel's professional, non-intrusive approach means couples feel like guests — not like they need to justify their presence.

  • 24-hour reception — late check-in for both guests seamlessly handled
  • Quiet side-street location adds to the discreet, private atmosphere
  • Applies to all room types — Superior, Deluxe, Suite

Pool and Breakfast — The Couple Stay Essentials

Outdoor Pool — Included for Both

The outdoor pool at Royal Ivory is one of the few in the budget 3-star Nana Bangkok area. For couples, the pool transforms the stay — an afternoon by the pool, morning swims before a day of sightseeing, or a relaxed evening in the water is a genuinely romantic and restorative experience. Bangkok's year-round heat (25-35°C) makes the pool valuable on every day of the stay. Free for both guests — no additional charge.

Breakfast for Two — Included

Breakfast is included in the room rate for all guests — meaning both people in a couple get breakfast without any extra charge. Served in the hotel's Thai restaurant, it's a proper restaurant breakfast rather than a grabbed coffee and toast. Starting the day with a sit-down breakfast together is a small but meaningful part of what makes Royal Ivory a genuinely comfortable couple's hotel.

Bangkok as a Couples Destination

Bangkok has extraordinary range for couples — from the cultural (temples, markets, river cruises) to the culinary (world-class Thai food at all price points) to the experiential (Thai massage, cooking classes, floating markets). Royal Ivory's BTS Nana location puts almost everything within easy reach.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Royal Ivory Nana Hotel good for couples?

Yes. Royal Ivory Nana Hotel is one of the best-value couples hotels in Bangkok's Nana and Sukhumvit area. Spacious rooms (32-45 sqm), outdoor pool for both guests, breakfast included for two, no extra guest charge, quiet side-street location, and a full Thai restaurant. Professional, non-intrusive staff. From 1,200 THB/night for a couple with breakfast.

Is there an extra charge for two guests at Royal Ivory?

No. Royal Ivory Nana Hotel does not charge extra for double occupancy. The room rate covers both guests, and breakfast is included for all guests in the room. There is no joiner charge, no extra person fee, and no separate registration requirement for partners or couples.

Which room is best for couples at Royal Ivory Bangkok?

For most couples, the Deluxe room (42+ sqm from 1,800 THB) is the best option — it has genuine living space, a kitchenette for storing drinks and snacks, breakfast included, and pool access. For special occasions or longer stays, the Suite (45 sqm from 2,800 THB) provides a full apartment-style experience with separate living area and bathtub. Both include breakfast and pool access.

What is the best romantic hotel near Sukhumvit Bangkok?

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel at 73 Sukhumvit Soi 4 provides an excellent romantic hotel experience at 3-star pricing. Thai-style decor, outdoor pool, full restaurant, spacious rooms, privacy-first policies, and central BTS location. The hotel's quiet Soi 4 position — close to everything but removed from street noise — creates the right atmosphere for a romantic Bangkok stay.
