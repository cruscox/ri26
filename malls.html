Bangkok Shopping Malls Guide from Royal IvoryTop 5 premier shopping destinations with easy BTS access from Nana station

Terminal 21 1.5km awayJust 1 BTS stop - World-themed mall in Asoke

Terminal 21 is Bangkok's newest shopping sensation, featuring a unique concept that takes visitors on a journey through 7 world cultures. Each of the mall's 7 floors represents a different city or country, complete with clever decorative elements and themed designs.

What Makes Terminal 21 Special:

  • Unique Theme: Each floor represents a different destination (Tokyo, London, Istanbul, San Francisco, Paris, Caribbean, Rome)
  • Instagram-Famous: 6th Floor features iconic San Francisco Golden Gate Bridge replica - perfect for photos
  • TripAdvisor Rank: #15 in Bangkok attractions
  • Food Courts: Very reasonably priced with diverse international options
  • Shopping: Countless boutique clothing lines and accessories
  • Cinema: SF Cinema for entertainment

Prime Location Advantage: Strategically positioned at the intersection of BTS Skytrain (Asok Station) and MRT Subway (Sukhumvit Station) in the heart of Sukhumvit's business district. Attracts tens of thousands of visitors daily - both tourists seeking photo opportunities and local shoppers.

From Royal Ivory: Take BTS from Nana to Asok (1 stop, 3 minutes) - Total travel time: 10 minutes including walk

Directions: Royal Ivory to Terminal 21 Learn More

Central World 2.5km away6th largest shopping complex in the world

Central World is a massive 180,000 square meter shopping complex featuring over 490 stores across 7 floors. As the 6th largest shopping complex globally, it's far more than just a mall - it's a Bangkok landmark and a complete lifestyle destination.

Size & Scope:

  • 490+ Stores: From high-street fashion to luxury brands
  • 7 Floors: Each dedicated to different shopping categories
  • 180,000 sq meters: One of Asia's largest shopping destinations
  • TripAdvisor Rank: #18 in Bangkok

Complex Layout - Three Zones:

  • Central World: Main zone with international brands
  • Zen Plaza: Fashion-focused section
  • Isetan: Japanese department store with premium goods

Dining & Entertainment:

  • 7th Floor: SF Cinema and fine dining restaurants
  • 6th Floor: Over 30 restaurants and fast food outlets
  • International cuisine from every continent

Strategic Hub: Central World connects three major shopping districts (Siam, Chitlom, and Pratunam) via BTS skywalks, making it the ultimate shopping hub of Bangkok.

From Royal Ivory: BTS Nana to Chit Lom (2 stops, 8 minutes) or to Siam (3 stops, 10 minutes) - Both stations connect to Central World via skywalk

Directions: Royal Ivory to Central World Learn More

MBK Center 3km awayBangkok's electronics hub - 100,000 visitors daily

MBK Center (Mah Boon Krong) is a 7-story shopping institution famous for mobile phones, electronics, and accessories. With over 100 outlets per floor, MBK attracts a staggering 100,000+ visitors every single day, making it one of Bangkok's busiest shopping destinations.

What You'll Find:

  • Mobile & Accessories: 100+ shops per floor specializing in phones, cases, and gadgets
  • Electronics: Cameras, laptops, computers, audio equipment
  • Fashion: Shoes, clothing, bags, and accessories
  • Home Goods: DÃ©cor, furniture, and household items
  • Souvenirs: Thai handicrafts and gifts

Dining Options:

  • 7th Floor: MBK Food Court with 100+ vendors
  • Ground Floor: Chain restaurants and cafes
  • Price Range: From 30 baht food court dishes to 500 baht restaurant meals

Shopping Strategy: Bargaining is expected! Start at half the asking price and negotiate. MBK is a paradise for electronics enthusiasts, with incredible variety and competitive prices.

From Royal Ivory: BTS Nana to Siam (3 stops, 10 minutes), then short walk to MBK via skywalk. Total journey: 15-20 minutes

Directions: Royal Ivory to MBK Learn More

Siam Paragon 3.5km awaySoutheast Asia's premier luxury shopping destination

Siam Paragon represents the pinnacle of luxury shopping in Southeast Asia. This flagship destination houses the world's most prestigious brands across multiple floors, complemented by fine dining restaurants, entertainment venues, and Thailand's largest aquarium.

Luxury Brands & Shopping:

  • International Luxury: Louis Vuitton, Hermès, Chanel, Gucci, Prada
  • Premium Fashion: Zara, H&M, Uniqlo, COS, and more
  • Electronics: Apple Store, Samsung flagship, and premium tech
  • Jewelry & Watches: Rolex, Cartier, Tiffany & Co.

Entertainment & Dining:

  • Siam Ocean World: Southeast Asia's largest aquarium (Basement level)
  • Fine Dining: Michelin-starred restaurants and premium cuisine
  • Cineplex: Luxury movie theater with VIP screening rooms
  • Gourmet Market: High-end imported foods and delicacies

Architecture & Design: The mall itself is a work of art, with stunning modern architecture, marble floors, designer lighting, and an atmosphere of luxury that matches its premium brand lineup.

From Royal Ivory: BTS Nana to Siam (3 stops, 10 minutes) - Direct connection via skywalk. Total journey: 15 minutes including walk

Directions: Royal Ivory to Siam Paragon Learn More

Pratunam Fashion District 2.5km awayBangkok's wholesale fashion paradise

Pratunam is Bangkok's fashion wholesale district, featuring multiple interconnected malls specializing in clothing, accessories, and textiles at incredibly low prices. Perfect for fashion lovers, boutique owners, and anyone looking for trendy clothes at wholesale prices.

Major Shopping Centers:

  • Platinum Fashion Mall: 6 floors, 2,000+ shops, air-conditioned comfort
  • Pratunam Market: Street market with outdoor stalls
  • Indra Square: 4 floors of fashion and accessories
  • Palladium World: IT products and electronics

What to Buy:

  • Women's Fashion: Dresses, tops, skirts, jewelry
  • Men's Clothing: Shirts, pants, suits, accessories
  • Bags & Accessories: Handbags, scarves, watches, sunglasses
  • Wholesale Quantities: Many shops offer better prices for bulk purchases

Shopping Tips: Bargaining is essential! Prices are often marked up 2-3x for tourists. The best deals are found by comparing prices across multiple shops before buying. Bring cash, as many vendors don't accept cards.

From Royal Ivory: BTS Nana to Chit Lom (2 stops, 8 minutes), then 5-minute walk to Platinum Fashion Mall. Or taxi directly (10-15 minutes depending on traffic)

Directions: Royal Ivory to Pratunam Learn More

Shopping Tips from Royal Ivory

  • BTS Day Pass: Buy a 1-day BTS pass (140 THB) for unlimited travel to all malls
  • Mall Hours: Most malls open 10 AM - 10 PM daily
  • Dining: Every mall has excellent food courts with 50-150 THB meals
  • Tourist Discount: Many malls offer tourist discount cards at information desks
  • Tax Refund: Keep receipts for VAT refund at airport (2000+ THB purchases)
  • Bargaining: Fixed prices in department stores, negotiable in smaller shops
