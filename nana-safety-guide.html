Nana Plaza Safety Overview

Yes, Nana Plaza is safe for tourists at night when proper precautions are followed. The complex operates with professional security standards, caters specifically to international visitors, and maintains a well-managed environment designed for tourist safety and comfort.

For Royal Ivory Hotel guests, safety is enhanced by our hotel's location just 200 meters away. You can walk to Nana Plaza in 3 minutes on well-lit Sukhumvit Road, enjoy the entertainment, and return safely to your comfortable room whenever you're ready.
