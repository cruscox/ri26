Contact Royal Ivory Nana HotelLocated in Sukhumvit Soi 4, near BTS Nana - Easy access to airport, shopping malls, and Bangkok attractions

From the Airport

  • Airport Rail Link + BTS: Take the Airport Rail Link to Phaya Thai station, transfer to BTS Sukhumvit Line to Nana station (700m from hotel). Most convenient option.
  • Hotel Limousine Service: Book our private airport transfer for THB 800 (approx. $26 USD) for stress-free pickup. Confirm landing time 24 hours in advance.
  • Taxi Meter: Available at airport Level 1. Journey takes 30-45 minutes depending on traffic. Cost approximately THB 300-400 plus expressway tolls.

From BTS Nana Station

  • Walking: Only 700 meters (8-10 minutes walk) from BTS Nana station. Exit 3, walk down Sukhumvit Soi 4.
  • Taxi: For guests with luggage, local taxis cost approximately THB 40-60 ($1-2 USD). Available at station.
  • Motorcycle Taxi: Quick and affordable option for guests with light luggage. Half the price of regular taxi.

By Private Car

  • GPS Coordinates: 13.7410° N, 100.5598° E
  • Address for Navigation: 73 Sukhumvit Soi 4, Khlong Toei, Bangkok 10110
  • Parking: Free parking available for hotel guests at the property.
