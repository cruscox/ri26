Sukhumvit Soi 4 Directory Overview

Sukhumvit Soi 4 is Bangkok's premier entertainment street, home to Nana Plaza and dozens of bars, restaurants, and essential services. Royal Ivory Hotel sits at the heart of this district, providing guests with unparalleled access to Bangkok's best nightlife and dining options.

Our central Soi 4 location means everything is within easy walking distance of your hotel room. From world-class entertainment at Nana Plaza (200m) to 24-hour pharmacies (150m) and international restaurants, you'll find everything needed for a perfect Bangkok experience.

🏨 Royal Ivory Hotel Advantages

Central Soi 4 location: Walking distance to all venues and services

No joiner fees: Guest-friendly policy for social visits

5-minute walk to Nana Plaza: Bangkok's premier entertainment complex

24-hour front desk: Local guidance and assistance available
