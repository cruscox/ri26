The Perfect Hotel for Your Group Bangkok Adventure

Planning a Bangkok trip with a group of friends? Royal Ivory Nana Hotel is built for exactly that experience. From spacious rooms that accommodate 2–4 guests comfortably, to a zero joiner charge policy, to the ability to book same-floor rooms for the whole group — everything about our hotel caters to groups of friends exploring Bangkok together.

We're located 200 meters from Nana Plaza entertainment complex and 700m (7-10 minutes walk) from BTS Nana Station, putting the entire city within easy reach. During the day: temples, markets, shopping, and cultural experiences. In the evening: Bangkok's famous nightlife at your doorstep. At night: spacious, quiet rooms where you actually sleep well.

Whether you're 2 friends or a group of 8, Royal Ivory Nana makes group travel in Bangkok easy, affordable, and genuinely enjoyable.

Group-Friendly Features at Royal Ivory Nana

1. No Joiner Charge — Saves Your Group Thousands

The single most important policy for group travelers: Royal Ivory Nana has no joiner charge. Period. Many Bangkok hotels near entertainment areas charge 500–1,000 THB per night to bring a visitor to your room. For a group of 5 staying 5 nights, that's 12,500–25,000 THB in unexpected fees at other hotels.

At Royal Ivory Nana: zero. No joiner fees, no restrictions, no questions. Your group saves money and enjoys full privacy.

2. Same-Floor Group Bookings Available

When you book for your group, contact us to request same-floor room placement. We accommodate group requests when rooms are available, making it easy for your friends to gather in the corridor, move between rooms freely, and maintain the social energy of your trip without elevator trips between floors.

3. Spacious Rooms — 32 to 45 sqm

Bangkok budget hotels often have cramped 18–22 sqm rooms. Royal Ivory Nana offers 32 to 45 sqm rooms — real space to store luggage, spread out, and share comfortably if needed. Group members won't feel trapped in their rooms.

4. Pool & Restaurant for Group Gathering

Royal Ivory Nana's outdoor swimming pool and restaurant create natural group gathering spaces. Start the day with group breakfast in the restaurant. Cool off at the pool before evening plans. Debrief from a night out with cold drinks at the bar. These communal spaces are what elevate a hotel stay from accommodation to a genuine group experience.

5. Multiple Room Keys & Flexible Access

Each room provides multiple key cards on request. This is essential for groups — members can come and go independently, meet separately, and return at different times without coordination stress. No curfews, no restrictions on timing.

6. Flexible Check-In for Group Arrivals

Groups often arrive at different times — some members on earlier flights, others later. Royal Ivory Nana's 24-hour front desk accommodates staggered arrivals. Rooms can be held and keys released as each group member arrives. No group has to wait in the lobby for hours.

Location: Perfect Base for Group Bangkok Exploration

Groups need a central location to coordinate. Royal Ivory Nana's position on Sukhumvit Soi 4 provides unmatched access for group Bangkok adventures:

Evening Entertainment (Walking Distance)

  • Soi 4 bars and restaurants: Directly outside the hotel
  • Sukhumvit nightlife strip: 5-minute walk

Day Activities (Via BTS Nana)

With BTS Nana just 2 minutes from the hotel, your group can split up during the day and reconvene easily. The BTS Skytrain covers most of Bangkok's key attractions without taxi costs or traffic stress.

Group Travel Tips for Bangkok

After hosting thousands of international groups, here's what makes a Bangkok group trip successful:

Before You Arrive

  • Book direct with hotel for same-floor requests and group rate
  • Get everyone's passport details for quick check-in
  • Agree on a budget before leaving — daily expenses vary widely
  • Download Grab app for shared taxi rides when BTS isn't convenient
  • Exchange currency at airport — rates better than city center

During Your Stay

  • Use hotel as your meeting point — easy for everyone to find
  • Restaurant breakfast together — great way to coordinate daily plans
  • Pool time — natural group gathering during afternoon hours
  • Check-in with front desk if anyone has questions — 24-hour service
  • Safe storage — leave valuables in room safe before evenings out

Evening Group Strategy

  • Start at Nana Plaza — 3 minutes from hotel, easy base
  • Groups can split between floors and venues, reconvene at entrance
  • Hotel is close enough to return and change, come back out
  • 24-hour front desk means any arrival time is welcome
  • No restrictions on return time — your schedule, your freedom

Frequently Asked Questions — Groups of Friends Bangkok

What is the best hotel for a group of friends in Bangkok?

Royal Ivory Nana Hotel in the Nana area is consistently rated the best hotel for groups of friends in Bangkok. Large rooms (32-45 sqm), no joiner charge, same-floor group bookings, outdoor pool, restaurant, and central location near Nana Plaza and BTS Nana make it the ideal group base.

How many people can stay in one room?

Superior Rooms (32 sqm): 1–2 guests. Deluxe Rooms (42 sqm): 2–3 guests. Presidential Superior & Deluxe (up to 45 sqm): up to 4 guests. Groups of friends typically book multiple rooms on the same floor for the best experience.

Can we request same-floor rooms for our group?

Yes. When booking, mention your group and request same-floor placement. We accommodate this when rooms are available. For guaranteed same-floor placement for large groups, contact us directly for group bookings where we can assign rooms in advance.

Is there a group discount at Royal Ivory Nana?

For groups of 5+ rooms, contact us directly for group pricing. We can offer preferential rates for confirmed group bookings. Email reservations@royalivory.com or call +66 2 656 7888 with your group dates and size.

Are there any restrictions for groups at Bangkok hotels?

At Royal Ivory Nana, there are no joiner charges, no curfews, no restrictions on guest arrivals or departures, and no judgment about your group's activities. Adult guests are treated with complete respect and privacy. We just ask all guests to respect other hotel guests' peace and comfort.
